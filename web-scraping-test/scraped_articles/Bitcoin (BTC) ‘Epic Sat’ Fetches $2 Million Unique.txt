Article Title: Bitcoin (BTC) ‘Epic Sat’ Fetches $2 Million Unique Ordinals Art Voiced by Grimes’ AI

Article Text:
A rare Bitcoin “epic satoshi” sold for 33.3 BTC has been used to inscribe an Ordinals asset with music and the AI-generated voice of pop artist Grimes, marking a unique intersection of crypto and art.
Bitcoin Ordinals project Book of Blob inscribed its artwork on the latest epic sat via inscription 70,634,325, which was inscribed on Sunday. The artwork, part of the “Spikes” collection, was created by pseudonymous Bitcoin enthusiast Nuro.
