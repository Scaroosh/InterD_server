Article Title: XRP Lawsuit 280M XRP Moved As Ripple CFO Files Declaration Under Penalty

Article Text:
In a riveting turn of events, the Ripple XRP vs. the U.S. SEC lawsuit has embarked upon a new turn today, following the May 13 filing of omnibus letter motions to seal key details regarding remedies-related documents. Ripple’s CFO (Chief Financial Officer), Jonathan Bilich, has filed a declaration in support of Ripple’s motion to seal the documents, as spotlighted by renowned lawyer James K. Filan.
